Company Leave Policy

1. Purpose
Employees are entitled to annual leave, sick leave, and compassionate leave according to local labor laws and company policy.

2. Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

3. Sick Leave
Up to 10 paid sick days per year with a medical certificate for absences over 3 days.

4. Confidentiality
Employees must follow confidentiality guidelines when handling sensitive company information.
